DevOps is a combination of software development and information technology operations that enables businesses to deliver applications at a faster pace.

Students will learn how to utilize the industry-standard tools, like GitHub, and data formats, eg XML, JSON, YAML, YANG, 
to drastically reduce IT overhead and remove manual tasks both of a mundance or complex nature.


There are 6 stages in the DevOps cycle:
1. Continuous Business Planning
2. Collaborative Development
3. Continual Testing
4. Sustained Release and Deployment
5. Ongoing Monitoring
6. Collaborative Customer Feedback & Optimization
