George Brown (1818-1880) was a 19th-century Scottish-born Canadian politician, 
newspaper publisher (he founded The Globe, precursor to The Globe and Mail) and 
was one of the Fathers of Confederation. An essential figure in the formation of Canada, 
Brown was a strong believer in public education
